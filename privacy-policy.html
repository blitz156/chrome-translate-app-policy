Privacy Policy for Quick Translator

Last updated: March 28, 2025

Information Collection and Use

Quick Translator is designed to respect your privacy while providing translation services. Here's how we handle your data:

  • We only process the text you specifically select for translation
  • We store your preferred language settings locally in your browser
  • We do not collect or store any personal information
  • We do not track your browsing history or activities

Data Processing

When you use Quick Translator:

  • Selected text is sent to translation service providers only for the purpose of translation
  • No selected text or translations are stored on our servers
  • All settings are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API

Permissions

Our extension requires minimal permissions to function:

  • storage: To save your language preferences locally

The extension uses content scripts to provide translation functionality on web pages, which does not require additional permissions.

Third-Party Services

We use translation APIs to provide translation services. These services receive only the text you select for translation and do not receive any personal information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@quicktranslator.app
